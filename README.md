### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I use the pitch detection?**  
A: Allow microphone access when prompted, then play your clarinet. Aimelo will show real-time pitch feedback on screen.

**Q: How do I use the posture detection?**  
A: Allow camera access when prompted, then position yourself so the camera is capturing you on the side view when practicing. The app will analyze your posture and provide feedback.

**Q: How do I track my progress?**  
A: Your practice sessions are automatically saved after each session. View your calendar and statistics in the Progress tab.

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes! All practice data is stored securely in your account. Camera and microphone are used for real-time analysis only - no recordings are stored. See our [Privacy Policy](https://www.termsfeed.com/live/bd9696c2-77b7-41fc-b60d-bf921a715eee).

**Q: Is Aimelo free?**  
A: Yes! Aimelo is completely free to download and use.

**Q: What iOS version do I need?**  
A: Aimelo requires iOS 16.0 or later.

Features

Everything you need to
practice with confidence

Aimelo combines AI-powered feedback with smart practice tools to help you improve faster — even without a teacher present.

🎯

Real-Time Pitch Detection

See exactly which note you're playing with instant visual feedback. Know immediately when you're sharp, flat, or right on target.

📸

AI Posture Analysis

Get real-time feedback on your playing posture using your phone's camera. Build proper technique from day one.

🎼

Sheet Music Upload

Scan or upload your own sheet music. Aimelo extracts the notes so you can practice along with guided feedback on accuracy.

📊

Progress Tracking

View daily accuracy charts, practice streaks, and session history. Watch your improvement over time with clear data.

🎵

Long Tone Practice

Build embouchure strength and breath control with an engaging bubble game that makes fundamentals fun.

🥁

Built-In Metronome

Keep time with an adjustable metronome built right into the app. Practice rhythm accuracy alongside pitch.

Our Mission

"Every student deserves quality practice feedback — not just those who can afford private lessons."

Yiren Jing, Founder — Arlington High School, MA

Aimelo was created by a high school clarinetist who saw firsthand how students without access to private instruction struggle to improve on their own. The app is and always will be completely free — because access to quality music education shouldn't depend on what you can afford.
